1 The ProblemProject Overview
In an industry dominated by complex tools and intimidating interfaces, TradeUp set out 
to reimagine stock trading for everyday users — without sacrificing depth for active 
traders. I led the UX efforts to design a platform that’s not just functional, but also 
frictionless and confidence-inspiring.
 Business Goals + UX Role
My Role: UX Designer — responsible for user research, user flows, 
wireframes, high-fidelity UI, and UAT.
KPIs: Improve onboarding completion rate, increase trade execution 
speed, and boost user retention.

4 SolutionFeatures introduced in Beta Launch
After uncovering that most users want to act fast on known stocks — and not dig 
through menus — I introduced a feature called Quick Trade .
W ith just a swipe, users could confirm trades without navigating to a separate screen.
F or active traders, I built in Smart Alerts that notify them about price triggers they 
define — directly on their lock screen .

These ideas weren’t just about cool features . 
 They came directly from user frustrations around slow workflows, missed opportunities, 
and the anxiety of “ missing the trade ! ”
