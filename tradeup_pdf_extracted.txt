Questions
1. Background & Motivation:
Can you tell me a bit about yourself 
and your experience with investing or 
trading stocks?
How do you usually trade or manage 
your investments today (e.g., mobile 
apps, websites, brokers)?
What motivates you to trade or invest 
in the stock market?
🔍 2. Current Experience & Pain Points:
What apps or platforms do you 
currently use for trading? Why did you 
choose them?
Can you walk me through your typical 
process when making a trade?
Are there any challenges you face 
while trading or managing your 
portfolio?
Have you ever felt frustrated or stuck 
while using trading apps? What 
caused that?
🏗 3. Needs & Expectations:
If you could design the perfect trading 
app, what would be the most 
important features for you?
How important is speed and ease of 
access when placing trades?
Do you prefer quick actions, or do you 
value detailed analysis before making 
a trade?
Would you like personalized insights, 
alerts, or educational content within 
the app?
📌 4. Decision-Making & Trust
What factors help you decide whether 
to trust a trading platform?
How do you typically research stocks 
or markets before trading?
Would you rely on in-app tools like 
charts, market news, or analyst 
ratings?
💬 5. Final Thoughts:
What’s one thing that would make 
trading easier or more enjoyable for 
you?
Is there anything you feel existing apps 
are missing or could improve upon?
Would you be interested in testing new 
trading features or prototypes in the 
future?
